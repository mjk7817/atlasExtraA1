CU Presents: Carmina Burana at Boettcher Hall

On Tuesday May 2nd, 2023 at 7pm, the CU Boulder Choirs alongside the Boulder Philharmonic Choir and the CU Symphony Orchestra will be performing Carmina Burana. The performance venue is Boettcher Concert Hall located in the Denver Center for Performing Arts off of 14th Street near CU Denver.
